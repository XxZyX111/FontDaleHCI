About FontDale

Built to deliver a modern premium mall experience

FontDale was envisioned by Franz Diego in 2023 and officially opened in 2025 as a premium lifestyle mall for urban shoppers who value design, convenience, and quality experiences.

Its two main wings, North Gallery and South Pavilion, are connected by the signature Lumen Skywalk, creating a recognizable identity and a smooth visitor journey.

Founder Vision

Franz Diego wanted FontDale to reflect contemporary architecture, lifestyle variety, and an elevated shopping atmosphere that stands apart from ordinary malls.

Unique Identity

The Lumen Skywalk visually unites both wings and turns circulation into part of the visitor experience — a signature architectural landmark.

Visitor Focus

FontDale balances retail discovery, dining, and events so visitors enjoy more than a standard mall trip — it is a complete lifestyle destination.

Contemporary by Design

Every page uses consistent color, spacing, and content grouping to support usability and readability.
